About the system

RING-0 is a smart community, class, and member coordination system built to help institutions, programs, and organized groups manage communication from one central place. Instead of relying on scattered phone calls, repeated announcements, and manual follow-up, the system gives owners one digital space where members can log in, receive updates, review information, and stay connected.

  • Owner-based private portals with dedicated access for each program or community.
  • Member dashboards that keep information organized and easy to reach.
  • Centralized communication that reduces confusion, delay, and repeated questions.
  • Scalable structure ready for multi-tenant SaaS deployment and subscription control.

Services

Our system provides a full digital service environment for owners who want to organize members, control updates, and deliver a professional information space without building custom software from scratch.

  • Private owner dashboard for managing members, comments, communication, and access.
  • Member portal where users log in to view updates and interact with the system.
  • Review and feedback tools that help owners understand member experience.
  • Custom tenant domains and subscription-driven access for each organization.
  • Cloud-based deployment with scalable storage space depending on subscription level.

Subscription plans can be structured around cloud space, number of members, and access level so each client pays only for the operational capacity they need.

Organization.

UNI-COT, the Union of Control & Optimization Technologies, was created by Stanslaus Emmanuel Mashamba the CEO of MC Core in 2025 to deliver high-tech solutions built for a faster, smarter, and more organized future.

From the beginning, the mission has been clear: build practical systems that remove friction from daily institutional operations and turn repeated manual pressure into smooth digital workflows.

Our vision is to create smarter environments where information moves through structured digital channels instead of stress, delay, and repetition.

One of the real problems we are solving is simple but costly: stopping members from calling owners every five minutes asking for class information, announcements, schedules, or basic updates.



With RING-0, those details live in one organized system where members can check for themselves anytime.
